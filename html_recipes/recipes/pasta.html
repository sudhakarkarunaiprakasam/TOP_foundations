Egg Pasta

Description

My variation of egg pasta based on the street food called 'Egg Fried Noodles' that I usually order from office cafeteria

Ingredients

  • Pasta (any type is fine) - 200 gms
  • Egg - 4 nos
  • Onion - 2 big, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 5 cloves, finely chopped
  • Spice Mix (Cinnamon, Clove, Cardamom) - As per your liking
  • Salt - As required
  • Vegetable Oil - As required

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta with adequate amount of water with salt. Cook as per instructions on the package
  2. Prepare the ingredients as per requirement
  3. Add oil to the pan and let it heat up for few minutes
  4. Add Spice Mix and toss for few seconds
  5. Add onion and let it fry till golden brown (i prefer carmelised onion taste, change as per your liking)
  6. Add Garlic and Tomato and saute till oil oozes out
  7. Add eggs into pan and let it fry as per your liking (I prefer well cooked eggs)
  8. 'Finally add boiled pasta and mix well. Once mixed, turn off the heat
  9. Enjoy
